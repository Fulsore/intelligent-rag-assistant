DIABETES MELLITUS

Definition:
Diabetes is a chronic metabolic disorder characterized by high blood glucose levels.

Symptoms:
- Frequent urination
- Excessive thirst
- Fatigue

Causes:
- Insulin resistance
- Pancreatic dysfunction

Treatment:
- Insulin therapy
- Metformin
- Diet control


HYPERTENSION

Definition:
Hypertension is a condition of persistently elevated blood pressure.

Symptoms:
- Headache
- Dizziness
- Blurred vision

Causes:
- Obesity
- Stress
- High salt intake

Treatment:
- ACE inhibitors
- Lifestyle modification


MALARIA

Definition:
Malaria is a mosquito-borne infectious disease caused by Plasmodium parasites.

Symptoms:
- Fever
- Chills
- Sweating

Treatment:
- Chloroquine
- Artemisinin-based therapy
