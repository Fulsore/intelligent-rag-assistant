DIABETES

Definition:
Diabetes is a chronic disease where the body cannot properly regulate blood glucose.

Symptoms:
- Frequent urination
- Excessive thirst
- Fatigue
- Weight loss

Causes:
- Insulin resistance
- Autoimmune destruction of pancreatic cells

Treatment:
- Insulin therapy
- Metformin
- Diet control


HYPERTENSION

Definition:
Hypertension is a condition of persistently high blood pressure.

Symptoms:
- Headache
- Dizziness
- Blurred vision

Causes:
- Obesity
- Stress
- High salt intake

Treatment:
- Lifestyle changes
- ACE inhibitors
